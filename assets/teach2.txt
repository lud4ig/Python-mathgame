BODMAS stands for Brackets, Orders, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction 
In all math equations, follow the BODMAS rule. 
1st: (B) - Solve the brackets first 
2nd: (O) - Handle any power or square roots 
3rd: (DM) - Handle division and multiplication from left to right (as per taught by the previous mage) 
4th: (AS) - Handle addition and subtraction from left to right (as per taught by the previous mage 
I have an example here, read through if you need to. 
Example: (8x5) - (35/5) x (7+3)
Step 1: Solve the brackets. 
        ~ 8 x 5 = 40 
        ~ 35 / 5 = 7 
        ~ 7 + 3 = 10 
        40 - 7 x 10 
Step 2: Solve the squares and roots. In this example there are no squares and roots, move on to step 3
Step 3: Solve the division and multiplication based on left to right sequence. 
        ~ 7 x 10 = 70
        40 - 70 
Step 4 : Solve the addition and subtraction. 
         ~ 40 - 70 = -30
So ... (8x5) - (35/5) x (7+3) = -30

5. (25+15) / 5 + (6x4) - 9 = ? (This should give you 23, else check the calculation above).
